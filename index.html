📋 Scenario

You work in the Al Ain Mall Customer Service Department which has received a large number of complaints from shoppers. You have been asked by the Al Ain Mall Director to review the complaints and suggest ways to solve the issues. You collected complaints from 158 shoppers and you surveyed staff about suggestions for improvements.

✍️ Task: Write a report making two recommendations to address the customer complaints.

Sample Answer – Al Ain Mall Report

Title

A Problem-Solving Report to Address Customer Complaints for Shoppers at Al Ain Mall Using a Customer Survey

Introduction

This report was requested by the Al Ain Mall Director. The purpose of this report is to review complaints received from shoppers and to recommend improvements. Data was collected from 158 shoppers using a survey. Staff were also surveyed about their suggestions for improvements. This report presents the findings and makes two recommendations to address the main issues.

Findings and Analysis

A survey of 158 shoppers was conducted to identify the main complaints. According to Chart 1, the most common complaint was parking, reported by 42 shoppers. The second most frequent issue was crowding, with 35 complaints. AC issues were reported by 31 shoppers, followed by cleanliness (28) and store variety (22).

Regarding staff suggestions in Chart 2, the most popular recommendation was more seating (30%), followed by more security (20%). Better signage was suggested by 18% of staff, a kids play area by 17%, and more events by 15%. These results suggest that both shoppers and staff agree that the comfort and organisation of the mall need to be improved.

Conclusions and Recommendations

In conclusion, the main issues at Al Ain Mall are insufficient parking and overcrowding. It is recommended, firstly, that the mall expand its parking facilities or introduce a multi-storey car park. This would reduce congestion and improve the shopping experience. Secondly, it is recommended that the mall add more seating areas throughout the building, as this was the top staff suggestion and would help shoppers feel more comfortable during their visit.

📖 Why this answer works: The title follows the full formula — Type + Purpose Verb + WHAT + WHO + WHERE + HOW. The introduction states the purpose, who requested the report, and what it includes. The findings describe both charts using specific numbers and percentages. The analysis sentence connects the two charts. The recommendations are clearly linked to the findings and include a justification.

Sample Answer – Ajman City Campus Report

Title

A Problem-Solving Report to Identify Suitable Campus Upgrades for Students at Ajman City Campus Using a Student Survey

Introduction

This report was requested by the Student Services Director at Ajman City Campus. The purpose of this report is to identify two campus upgrades based on student suggestions. A survey of 260 students was conducted to collect data on preferred upgrades. Estimated costs for each upgrade were also calculated. This report presents the findings and makes two recommendations.

Findings and Analysis

According to Chart 1, the most popular suggestion was a new sports facility, with 90 students selecting this option. The second most common suggestion was a library extension, chosen by 75 students. An improved student lounge was suggested by 55 students, while a new cafeteria and additional parking received 25 and 15 votes respectively.

Chart 2 shows the estimated costs of these upgrades. The sports facility is the most expensive option at AED 350,000, followed by the library extension at AED 250,000. The student lounge upgrade is estimated at AED 120,000. These results suggest that while the most popular upgrade is costly, the library extension offers a balance between student demand and affordable cost.

Conclusions and Recommendations

In conclusion, students at Ajman City Campus most want improvements to sports and library facilities. It is recommended, firstly, that the campus invest in a library extension at an estimated cost of AED 250,000. This was the second most popular choice and offers good value compared to the sports facility. Secondly, it is recommended that the campus upgrade the student lounge, as this is a more affordable option at AED 120,000 and would benefit a large number of students daily.

📖 Why this answer works: The title follows the full formula — Type + Purpose Verb + WHAT + WHO + WHERE + HOW. The introduction includes who requested the report, the purpose, and the method. The findings use specific numbers from both charts. The analysis sentence compares cost vs popularity. The recommendations are clearly justified with cost and student demand evidence.
